Description

Pokemon Fighter is a small battle simulation of the already known pokemon games. 1 pokemon ( monster ) fights against 1 other pokemon, the winner is decided when one of the pokemons have their health depleted.

Assignment

My assignment was to make a battle simulation that is similar to the experience we have when playing the game itself, but build it using Javascript. You were allowed to use different monsters in order to so. Though I've always been a huge pokemon fan, so this was an opportunity to see how these fights really work.
