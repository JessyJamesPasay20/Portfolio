Description

During my 6 month minor abroad in Finland, I made an animation to finalize my time there. During my time there I've learned alot about Adobe After effects and Premiere Pro, so this video serves as a display of my skills and art style.

Assignment

For my Film & TV course, I was tasked to pick a subject within the film industry and thoroughly research it over the span of a month. During my stay in Finland, I had been practicing a lot with Adobe after effects, so I decided to go all out in order to make something I could be proud of. So for my final project, I made a credit sequence of my experience in Helsinki. The video is about 40 seconds long, and the one of the main points of motivation was to find out more about my own art style. I think I've achieved something thats closed to what I desire from my desgins.
