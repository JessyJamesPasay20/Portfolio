Description

RNGRZ is social hub where people can dine and work, they can either sit at the variety of tables in the main area or rent rooms for meetings. It's a new establishment, and need new branding for their grand opening. Reyez! Alkmaar is a direct partner to RNGRZ located in the same building.

Assignment

The assignment for Reyez! was creating the entire branding of RNGRZ, my task in this was creating a style for workclothing, business cards, and prints like childrens coloring stencils, brochures, menu cards a mascott for the website. This was done during my internship, two other designers at the firm worked along side my on their own responsibilities.
