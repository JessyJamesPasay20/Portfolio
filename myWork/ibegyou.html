Description

A design for the full lyrics of one my favorite songs ever : I beg you, from Aimer. The focus was to make a design that matches the songtext, so the reader can visually understand what the lyrics really mean.

I made the design for mobile sizes only. You can find full documentation of my process & storyboards of animations by clicking on the button below.

Assignment

This was my final assignment for Design class in Year 2. The assignment was to pick a song you really like, and think of a design the compliments the lyrics. You're asked to do extensive research in regards to what you're trying to make. Further more you have to make an animation the further supports the design.
