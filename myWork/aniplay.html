Aniplay

Back Website

Aniplay is a concept targetting an audience that enjoys watching Anime together with friends. The problem I'm solving with this issue, is that people need to use multiple different apps or products to watch shows online with friends. My way to solve this, is by combining it all in a single app. So friends or strangers can watch their favorite shows while being able to speak and chat with eachother on the same screen.

This is currently a design only, I personally think it's a great idea that has not been implemented well on current apps & websites. So this is my take on solving this issue.

Year

2022

Client

Hogeschool van Amsterdam

Assignment

This was my first project ( Multi device design ) during my second year attending the HVA. I was tasked to think of a concept that is consistent and usable on multiple devices seemlessly.
