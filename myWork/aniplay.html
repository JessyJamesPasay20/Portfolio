Aniplay

Back Website

Asiandam is an app that provides Asian exchange students with an abundance of information about the important things such as Travelling, grocery shopping in Holland. And a large list of Asian restaurants and stores so the students can get used to Holland, without feeling homesick.

Currently the website is only designed for mobile sizes, I have yet to make it responsive for all sizes. Something for a later date. Scroll down for the mobile images!

Year

2021

Client

Hogeschool van Amsterdam

Assignment

This was my first project on the HVA, it was about making up your own topic for helping exchange students getting used to Holland just a bit easier. In my case I was interested in making something for the Asian students, as I am an Asian myself! This project scored me an 8/10
