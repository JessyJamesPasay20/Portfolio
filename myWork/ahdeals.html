Albert Heijn Deals

Back Design

In collaboration with the Albert Heijn, I've researched & created a solution to further motivate customers to make use of their deals platforms. It is an application where users can easily order product deals in store without the hassle of doing online research or complicated steps. A true friend to your wallet!

Year

2023

Client

Albert Heijn

Assignment

This was my final project for the second year of studying at the HVA, this time we were appointed to solve a problem that AH has created for us. Which is motivating users to make use of their deals system, and digitalize their paper format. They had given us 6 weeks to indivdually do research, design, iterate & present.

Before this project we had always received similar assignments, though with a non existent client. This time it was a real one, with hopes of useful results that they could use in solving their issue.

One of the bigger challenges during this project was leading the project indivdually, and making sure your documentation & results were factual / traceable. Looking back I've truly learned alot, and have made me improve as a designer.
